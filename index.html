What is our goal?

We want your children to have fun!

We want every child's daily learning fun and an adventure they will always come back for more.

food

Like you parents, we would like to make sure that your child would have a balanced diet and be active inside and out of the school

Feel free

Your child would always be heard and be seen. Your child would always have the chance to explore, particularly the outside environment

friends

Of course our aim is to make sure that they play and learn together with their peers. Have fun with their teachers and friends in school

What we offer:

Play

Play is a vital part of our dialy activities. Your child will always have a chance to play both indoors and outdoors. Our school have plenty of space for them to explore

Health

Our lunch and snacks is thought off and well prepared to make sure that your child's meal is always healthy and safe

Gym time and circle time

We always have circle time be it inside our outside of our school to ensure that each child is heard. We also offer gym activities to the children, where they are taught by a certified gym teacher in how to jump, roll and all fun activities!

Forest Play

Our school is located near a forest where the children get to play with nature and at the same time explore the outdoors with their teacher
